Spaghetti

Description

This is another great pasta dish that uses store-bought sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Store-bought tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to directions on package.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
  3. Add the carrot.
  4. Add the garlic and onion.
  5. Add tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add italian seasoning.
  7. Serve over spaghetti.
