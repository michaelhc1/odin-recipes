Fried Rice

Description

This simple fried rice dish is the perfect lunch or dinner when you need a quick and easy, but also healthy, meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Veggies (carrots, onions, peas, corn, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
  2. Add the veggies. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the rice. Mix well.
  4. Add soy sauce, sesame oil, chopped green onion, and sesame seeds.
