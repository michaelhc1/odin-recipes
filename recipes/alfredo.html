Alfredo

Description

This is a simple alfredo pasta dish that uses store-bought alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • Store-bought alfredo sauce
  • Pasta of choice (e.g. Fettucini, penne, scoobi-doo)
  • Veggies (e.g. Broccoli, carrot, onion)
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to directions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet.
  3. Add the garlic and veggies. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the alfredo sauce. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add cooked pasta and mix well.
